Swords Makerspace

Introduction:

Swords Makerspace is an online makerspace (at the moment with plans for a shared space) based in the suburbs of Swords Co. Dublin, Ireland. We are trying to find a shared space where members have a place to be creative and work on their projects in an environment that is both inspiring and supportive of both new and old technologies

What we will do?:

We will teach Science, Technology, Engineering, and mathematics (STEM). This covers coding and electronics also. We will also have social nights for our members and the wider community.

What we will need?:

Safe and accessible place for our members and the wider community in Swords. Ideally light industrial/workshop space with facilities. We also need funding for the rent, employment and equipment.
